# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential clients and collaborators visiting my personal link page. They want a quick, polished impression of who I am as a web developer and easy access to my work, social presence, and contact channels. The page is their first touchpoint before deciding to hire or work with me.

## Product Purpose

A personal link-in-bio page that replaces generic tools like Linktree. It exists to present a professional, distinctive identity in a single glance. Success means the visitor leaves with a clear sense of my craft and clicks through to at least one of my links.

## Brand Personality

Simple, elegant, confident. The tone is understated but purposeful. Nothing flashy, nothing excessive. Every element earns its place.

## Anti-references

Avoid the generic Linktree template look. No visual clutter, no loud colors, no gratuitous animation. Not trying to be "different for the sake of different" but deliberately refined.

## Design Principles

1. **Confident restraint.** Fewer elements, better executed. Whitespace is a feature.
2. **First glance tells the story.** A visitor should understand the page's purpose and the person behind it within two seconds.
3. **Calm professionalism.** Warm, easy-on-the-eyes colors. Nothing abrasive. The page feels like a handshake.
4. **Mobile-first, mobile-perfect.** Most visits are from phones. The experience on a 375px screen is the primary target.
5. **Craft is visible in the details.** Typography, spacing, hover states, transitions. The page itself is a portfolio piece.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Good contrast ratios. Colors that are comfortable for extended viewing. No flashing or rapid motion. Readable font sizes at all screen widths.
